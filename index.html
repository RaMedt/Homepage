Hello there!

New buzzwords are ten a penny in business and IT. Figuring out the relevant and achievable aspects in innovation, turning them into value for our customers and ensuring their global delivery is my responsibility for more than a decade now.

A strong entrepreneurial spirit is required to develop and implement business that is contradictionary and some times disruptive to our traditional IT service organization. Fortunately my employer Atos, one of the big global IT service providers, is supporting or even demandig this from his management. In my role as Vice President SaaS, I drive the development of business applications with complex IT and service process requirements. That's why Cloud Computing, with its technical, organizational and commercial concepts, is the foundation, combined with decades of experience and capabilities to add value to my clients and my employer.
